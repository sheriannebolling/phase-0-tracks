"Sloths!"

If you never have seen a sloth you might think of some terrifing creature. You might imagine it lurking slowly in the dark about to attack! Here are some reasons not to fear sloths:

  1. They eat leaves, not humans!
  2. They move so slow that algae grows on them. You can...run away.
  3. Their lives revolve around sleeping.

Now that you aren't scared of sloths, you should know that they are quite lovable. If you don't believe me here are some reasons to love sloths:

  • They are adorable when they are babies!
  • They are adorable as adults!
  • They are always adorable!
